Mobile Legends

Mobile Legends bang bang or also known as MLBB is an online multiplayer game. Each match consists 2 teams.In oredr for you to win a match, you must destroy the enemy's base. The game is available for free and is only monetized through in-game purchases such as characters and skins. Each player controls a distinct "Hero" character with unique abilities and traits. The basic objective of heroes is defined by six roles:"Tank," "Marksman," "Assassin," "Fighter," "Mage," and "Support." These positions define each player's responsibilities for their respective teams. Players can also customize their heroes' builds, which include in-game equipment and emblems.
